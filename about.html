About Me

I’m Marco Pienaar, a multi-instrumentalist who plays electric and acoustic guitar, drums, and performs heavy vocals found in metal music—I even write my own songs. I enjoy keeping active with regular exercise, and when I’m not making music, you’ll likely find me gaming. I’m a fan of titles like League of Legends and CS:GO, and I have a soft spot for retro gaming. I love emulating old consoles like the PS2 and GBA, and I get a kick out of modding their operating systems to tweak and enhance the experience. Whether it’s shredding a riff or diving into a virtual world, I’m all about exploring and creating.
